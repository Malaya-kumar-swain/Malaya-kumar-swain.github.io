01 Approach

How I build AI at national scale

Frontier capability is easy to demo and hard to trust. My job is to make AI dependable — for 400 million people, on the cheapest phone, in their own language.

I've spent 21 years at the intersection of data, cloud, and product — from Fortune 500 warehousing and Big-4 banking regulation to today's agentic, multilingual, voice-first consumer AI. That range is deliberate: shipping AI at scale is as much about governance, latency, and organisational alignment as it is about models.

JIO PLATFORMSAgentic Consumer AI2026 – Present

JioBharatIQ — an agentic AI for a billion-scale audience

Challenge

Deliver a trustworthy agentic AI assistant to 400M+ subscribers across 22 Indian languages — voice and text — on low-end 2G/3G devices, across an open ecosystem of first- and third-party partners.

Decision

Architected a React Native + JDS client with MCP tool orchestration, LiveKit WebRTC encrypted voice, an AG-UI real-time widget renderer, and a 4-layer guardrail framework (partner governance, schema validation, CSP sandboxing, signed OTA bundles) for zero-trust across partners.

Outcome

Progressive Response Design guarantees a 2-second signal on 2G; JioAirSync OTA ships P0 patches in under 4 hours with auto-rollback. Leading a 17-member CoreUX team through a 3-phase rollout to GA.

APPTAD · FORTUNE 500GenAI MDM on AWS2023 – 2026

AEVAH — retiring legacy MDM with GenAI

Challenge

A Fortune 500 manufacturer was locked into legacy Informatica MDM — expensive to license and painfully slow, with source onboarding taking 6+ weeks.

Decision

Owned vision and delivery of a GenAI-powered MDM platform on AWS: a zero-loss migration of 50M+ golden records across a raw → curated → golden S3 architecture on Spark/EMR, with LLM profiling + RAG + MCP source onboarding and OpenSearch vector entity resolution.

Outcome

Decommissioned Informatica, redirecting 8+ source systems to metadata-driven pipelines with guardrails (PII masking, prompt-injection prevention, RBAC) — across 6 SAFe PI cycles and a 30+ member team.

Re-platforming a bank's data estate to the cloud

Challenge

ANZ's Global Asset Finance ran on a legacy Teradata warehouse — analyst queries took 45 seconds, APRA regulatory compliance was manual, and delivery spanned a 100+ member multi-vendor ecosystem across three continents.

Decision

Led the Teradata-to-BigQuery migration with a 3-zone architecture — partitioning and clustering, 4-tier IAM, Policy Tags for PII, and Authorized + Materialized Views — plus a collections analytics platform for predictive EMI recovery.
